Privacy Policy for Screen Translator

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Screen Translator ("the app") is developed by the app developer identified in the Google Play listing. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, collects, uses, stores, and shares.

Information We Handle

Information created by the app

  • The app creates an anonymous Firebase Authentication account so points, Premium status, and translation history can be synchronized. No name, email address, phone number, or social-media account is required.
  • The app uses the Android ID to associate a device with its synchronized points and account data. It is used only for synchronization, fraud prevention, and account support.
  • Translation history may include recognized source text, translated text, language selections, timestamps, and optional captured images or image references used by the translation feature.
  • Settings, pending points, translation preferences, and user-provided AI API keys are stored locally on the device. API keys are protected with Android Keystore when available.

Screen content

The app requires user approval through Android MediaProjection before it captures screen content. Captured content is processed to recognize and translate text. Depending on the selected engine:

  • ML Kit can process OCR and translation on the device after its language model is available.
  • Google Online translation sends recognized text to Google's translation service.
  • AI translation sends the text, and when vision translation is enabled the relevant image content, to the AI provider selected by the user. The selected provider's privacy policy also applies.

The app does not capture the screen before the user grants MediaProjection permission and does not intentionally collect audio, contacts, location, SMS, or phone-call data.

Advertising

The app uses Google Mobile Ads for banner, rewarded, and App Open Ads. Google and its advertising partners may process device information, advertising identifiers, diagnostics, and ad interaction information to provide, measure, and protect advertising. The app gives points only after the user voluntarily chooses to watch a rewarded ad and the ad reports a reward event.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • provide screen OCR and translation;
  • synchronize points, Premium status, and translation history;
  • prevent duplicate or fraudulent point rewards;
  • save user settings and language models on the device;
  • show and measure advertising for non-Premium users;
  • troubleshoot errors, maintain security, and respond to support requests.

We do not sell personal or sensitive user data.

Service Providers and Sharing

Information may be processed by service providers needed to operate the app:

  • Firebase Authentication, Cloud Functions, and Firestore for anonymous authentication, synchronization, points, and history;
  • Google Mobile Ads for advertising;
  • Google translation services when Google Online translation is selected;
  • The AI provider selected by the user when AI translation is selected;
  • Google ML Kit for on-device OCR and translation models.

The app does not share screen content with an AI provider unless the user selects that provider and starts a translation that requires it. The privacy practices of those providers are controlled by their own policies.

Retention and Deletion

Local settings and local history remain on the device until the user clears them, uninstalls the app, or uses Android's app-data controls. Cloud-synchronized history, points, and anonymous account records are retained while needed to provide synchronization and support.

To request deletion of the anonymous account and associated cloud data, contact aspirin106@proton.me from the email address you want us to use for the request. We will verify the request and delete or anonymize data that is not required for security, fraud prevention, legal, or accounting purposes. Deleting local app data may also be required to remove data stored only on the device.

Security

We use HTTPS for network communication and Android Keystore protection for locally stored AI API keys when supported by the device. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so users should not submit information they are not comfortable sending to a selected translation provider.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when the app's data practices or legal requirements change. The updated version will show a new "Last updated" date and will be made available at the public Privacy Policy URL used in the Google Play listing.
